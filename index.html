Biography

Hi, I am Sadman Sadeed Omee.

I am a Ph.D. Student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of South Carolina. I am conducting my research in the Machine Learning and Evolution Laboratory where I work as a Graduate Research Assistant under the supervision of Dr. Jianjun Hu. I am born and raised in Bangladesh. I got my B.S. in Computer Science and Engineering from Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology. My aim is to become an effective and impactful Deep Learning Researcher.
